Project Expense Sheet for a 28,000 sq ft Aquaponic Greenhouse Overview: The project involves constructing a controlled environment greenhouse with an aquaponics system and an organic catfish farm. The greenhouse will be capable of growing 1 million pounds of leafy greens (such as kale, mustard greens) annually. The project also includes a research area inside the greenhouse. The expenses involved in the project are listed below: Greenhouse Construction Foundation: $5,000 Framing: $20,000 Insulation: $10,000 Glazing: $25,000 HVAC System: $50,000 Plumbing and Electrical: $30,000 Automation and Control System: $20,000 Lighting: $10,000 Miscellaneous (tools, equipment, permits, etc.): $15,000 Total: $175,000 Aquaponics System and Organic Catfish Farm Fish tanks: $20,000 Plumbing and filtration system: $15,000 Pumps and aeration equipment: $10,000 Organic catfish fingerlings: $5,000 Fish food: $3,000 Grow beds and media: $25,000 Irrigation system: $5,000 Total: $83,000
